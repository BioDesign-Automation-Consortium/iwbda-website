Sponsorship Opportunities

BDAC's IWBDA sponsorship opportunities are available to your company! IWBDA offers many special opportunities for your company to connect with your audience. Work with us to design a custom sponsorship opportunity, such as sponsoring:
  • A reception for IWBDA attendees at a local venue the night before the workshop opens.
  • Scholarships to enable students to attend IWBDA.
  • A featured space within IWBDA, such as the food area, registration desk, exhibition area, etc.
All net proceeds from IWBDA support the ongoing mission and work of BDAC.
